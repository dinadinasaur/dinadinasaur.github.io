A little bit about me...

I recently graduated with and M.S. in Computer Science. I concentrated my studies on the art of software engineering, while also getting to learn about the importance of data and emerging technologies (particularly in the field of Artificial intelligence).

Prior to my graduate studies in Computer Science, I dedicated my undergraduate career to strengthening my critical thinking skills through the study of Philosophy. I explored ethics and logic, which ultimately guided my decision to transtion to tech.

The transition from Philosophy to Computer Science was a difficult, though rewarding journey. Along the way, I have found that people are always willing to help. I have had many mentors along the way and am lucky to have a strong support group of women in STEM.
