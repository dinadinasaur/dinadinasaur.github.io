Hi, I'm Dina! Welcome to my website!

I suppose this website is an online resume or CV of sorts. So here is a look at what I have been up to the past couple of years...

Educational background...

I recently graduated with and M.S. in Computer Science. I concentrated my studies on the art of software engineering, while also getting to learn about the importance of data and emerging technologies (particularly in the field of Artificial intelligence).

Prior to my graduate studies in Computer Science, I dedicated my undergraduate career to strengthening my critical thinking skills through the study of Philosophy. I explored ethics and logic, which ultimately guided my decision to transtion to tech.

The transition from Philosophy to Computer Science was a difficult, though rewarding journey. Along the way, I have found that people are always willing to help. I have had many mentors along the way and am lucky to have a strong support group of women in STEM.

Some more fun experiences...

My career in comedy started when I was 18 years old. I was an intern at a small club on the Upper West Side of Manhattan. There a found a mentor who has continued to guide me in this male dominated field.

I got to travel to Scotland for the Edinburugh Fringe Festival. There I met comedians from all over the world. After performing stand-up comedy for a few years, I realized that I wanted to shift my focus to working behind the scenes. I contributed to the websites of several comedians including Francis Ellis, Aaron McCann, and Ashley Austin Morris.

Currently, I am the Social Media Manager for Maddy Smith. I joined her team over a year ago, so I have been able to watch her career growth in real-time.
